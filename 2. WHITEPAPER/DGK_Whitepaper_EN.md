# Digital Gateway Key (DGK-S)

## Abstract

DGK-S is the Solana-native issuance of Digital Gateway Key, designed as a fixed-supply SPL token
representing digital access, liquidity coordination, and long-term structural value.

## Tokenomics

Total Supply: 25,000,000 DGK-S
Decimals: 9
Mint Authority: Revoked after mint
Freeze Authority: Revoked after mint

## Distribution Model

*   Treasury Vault
*   Ecosystem incentives
*   Liquidity provisioning
*   Community growth

## Governance

Supply is fixed. No inflation. No hidden mint.
